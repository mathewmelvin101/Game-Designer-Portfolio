Crumbs Of Survival

Crumbs of survival is a quirky action-adventure game where players hunt and capture zombies in a lighthearted, post-apocalyptic world. Armed with guns we have to lure zombies out of hiding, snag them, and then use them as ingredients to create bizarre cookies. These cookies are sold to eager customers, generating coins to upgrade your gear, expand your production, and unlock new maps filled with unique zombie types. Blending action, resource management, and humor, the game turns zombie hunting into a fun and addictive cookie-making business venture.
